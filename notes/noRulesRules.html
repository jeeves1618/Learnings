No Rules Rules

Section One

First Steps to a Culture of Freedom and Responsibility

1. First Build Up Talent Density...
a. A Great Workplace Is Stunning Colleagues
2. Then Increase Candor...
a. Say What You Really Think (with Positive Intent)
b. 4A feedback: Aim to assist (giving), Actionable(giving), Appreciate(receiving), Accept or Discard(receiving)
3. Now Begin Removing Controls...
a. Remove Vacation Policy
b. Remove Travel and Expense Approvals
c. No "vacation policy", not "no vacation" policy

Section Two

Next Steps to a Culture of Freedom and Responsibility

4. Fortify Talent Density...
a. Pay Top of Personal Market
b. When recuiters call, encourage employees to ask "how much?"
5. Pump Up Candor...
a. Open the Books
b. Get rid of closed offices, "secratary" guards and locks
c. Whisper failures and shout successes
6. Now Release More Controls...
a. No Decision-making Approvals Needed
b. Don't seek to please your boss
c. Farm for dissent

Section Three

Techniques to reinforce a culture of Freedom and Responsibility

7. Max up Talent Density...
a. The keeper test...
b. If I were thinking of leaving, how hard would you work to change my mind?
c. A family is staying together regardless of performance
d. At work, we are not a family, we are a professional sports team
8. Max up Candor...
a. A circle of feedback
b. 360 degree feedback
c. Start... Continue... Stop...
9. And Eliminate Most Controls...
a. Lead with context not control

Section Four

Going global

10. Bring it all to the world
a. Add another A to 4A. Adapt
b. In less direct cultures make feedback more formal
c. Make feedback providing part of job description when it isn't forthcoming in cultures
