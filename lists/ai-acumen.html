Curated Coursera Courses for Gen AI Acumen

1. Introduction to GenAI & Prompt Engineering

  • GenAI for Everyone An accessible introduction to Generative AI, covering its capabilities and applications in daily life.
  • Generative AI: Prompt Engineering Basics Focuses on the fundamentals of crafting effective prompts to interact with AI models.

2. Writing & Communication Enhancement

  • Writing with Generative AI Teaches how to utilize GenAI tools to improve writing tasks, including drafting and editing.
  • Microsoft 365 with Generative AI Demonstrates integration of GenAI within Microsoft Office tools to boost productivity.

3. Programming Assistance

  • Generative AI for Software Developers Explores the use of GenAI in software development, including code generation and debugging.
  • AI Agents and Agentic AI in Python Covers the creation of AI agents using Python, emphasizing practical applications.

4. Personal Mentoring in Technology & Management

  • Navigating Generative AI for Leaders Designed for leaders to understand and implement GenAI strategies within organizations.
  • Generative AI for Executives and Business Leaders Focuses on strategic decision-making and leveraging GenAI for business innovation.

5. Content Creation & Knowledge Sharing

  • Generative AI and ChatGPT for K-12 Educators While aimed at educators, it provides insights into creating engaging content using GenAI.
  • Generative AI Automation Teaches automation of tasks and content creation processes using GenAI tools.
